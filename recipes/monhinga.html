Monhinga

Description

Monhinga is one of the Burmese famous dishes.

Most people love to eat Monhinga as breakfast

Ingredients

For the Soup Base

  • cat fish(skin on)
  • lemongrass,smashed and tied together with an outer leaf
  • garlic cloves
  • dried red chillies
  • whole tumeric roots
  • fish sauce
  • cilantro
  • water
<

Steps

  1. Boil the fish
  2. Simmer
  3. Remove and Shred Fish
  4. Add Spices
  5. Blend
