Tea Leaf Salad

Description

Tea Leaf Salad (Lat Phat Tote) is one of the traditional side dish for Burmese people.

People love to eat with some tea

It is also a traditional to treat guests with tea leaf salad at home especially in some villages

Ingredients

  • Tea Leaf
  • Fried Garlic
  • Dried Shrimp
  • Garlic
  • Fried Bean
  • Fish Sauce
  • Tomato
  • Bean Oil
  • Chilli

Steps

  1. To make the dressing, steep the tea leaves in hot water for 3 minutes. Drain well, pressing out excess water.
  2. Cool the leaves to room temperature.
  3. In a small food processor, whirl the garlic, ginger, and salt to a minced state.
  4. Add the tea leaves and whirl to a fine texture
  5. Add the oil, vinegar, and fish sauce, then pulse to combine. You should have a scant Â½ cup.
  6. To assemble salad, make a bed of lettuce on a rimmed serving platter
  7. Drizzle on the fish sauce or sprinkle on the salt
  8. Like flower petals, arrange piles of tomatoes, chile, fried garlic, peanuts, roasted chickpeas, sunflower seeds, sesame seeds, and shrimp powder.
  9. Scrape the dressing into the center.
  10. At the table, squirt on half of the lime wedges and toss the salad. If diners want more tartness, let them add extra lime juice.
